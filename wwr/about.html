History

Jarabacoa Rafting Travel was born from a deep love for the mountains, rivers, and the adventurous spirit that flows through Jarabacoa — the heart of the Dominican Republic’s ecotourism.

Through dedication, teamwork, and a love for the outdoors, Jarabacoa Rafting Travel has become one of the region’s most trusted and exciting adventure companies.
