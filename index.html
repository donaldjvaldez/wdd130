Dominican Republic

Known for its beautiful beaches, lush mountains, and lively culture, it is the most visited destination in the Caribbean. The country has a rich history, being the site of the first permanent European settlement in the Americas. Its people are known for their warmth, hospitality, and love of music and baseball. The economy is driven by tourism, agriculture, and services, and its capital city, Santo Domingo, is one of the oldest cities in the New World.
